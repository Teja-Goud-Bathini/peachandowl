ACADEMIC PROJECTS

    M.E. Project : # STUDY OF ACOUSTIC SOURCE LOCALIZATION FOR PLANAR ARRAYS

    The earlier works in the area of acoustic source localization mostly used non-planar arrays. In this project, an attempt had been to extend a linear closed-form source localization algorithm based on time difference of arrivals (TDOAs) for planar arrays. The robustness of the source location estimate using the least squares and total least squares approach, with respect to the error in TDOAs, microphone locations is studied. This project also deals with TDOA estimation.
